# 🌡️ Temperature Converter & 🎲 Number Guessing Game

This repository contains two beginner-friendly **Java console projects** that demonstrate problem-solving and core programming concepts.

---

## 📌 Projects

### 1️⃣ Temperature Converter
- Converts between **Celsius**, **Fahrenheit**, and **Kelvin**.  
- Demonstrates:
  - Methods
  - Scanner for input
  - Conditional statements  

✅ Example:  
25.00 °C = 77.00 °F
25.00 °C = 298.15 K




---

### 2️⃣ Number Guessing Game
- Random number generated between **1–100**  
- User has **10 attempts** to guess  
- Provides hints: `"Too High"`, `"Too Low"`  
- Ends with **Congratulations 🎉** or shows correct number  

✅ Example:  
=== Number Guessing Game ===
Guess the number between 1 and 100!
You have 10 attempts.
Enter your guess: 50
Too low! Attempts left: 9

🧩3. Sudoku Solver
This repository contains a beginner-friendly Java console project that demonstrates backtracking, recursion, and 2D array manipulation.

📌 Project
Sudoku Solver

Solves standard 9x9 Sudoku puzzles automatically.

Uses recursive backtracking to fill empty cells while following Sudoku rules.

Prints the board neatly with 3x3 subgrid separators.

Informs if the puzzle is unsolvable.

✅ Example:

Original Sudoku:
5 3 . | . 7 . | . . . 
6 . . | 1 9 5 | . . . 
. 9 8 | . . . | . 6 . 
------+-------+------
8 . . | . 6 . | . . 3 
4 . . | 8 . 3 | . . 1 
7 . . | . 2 . | . . 6 
------+-------+------
. 6 . | . . . | 2 8 . 
. . . | 4 1 9 | . . 5 
. . . | . 8 . | . 7 9 

Solved Sudoku:
5 3 4 | 6 7 8 | 9 1 2 
6 7 2 | 1 9 5 | 3 4 8 
1 9 8 | 3 4 2 | 5 6 7 
------+-------+------
8 5 9 | 7 6 1 | 4 2 3 
4 2 6 | 8 5 3 | 7 9 1 
7 1 3 | 9 2 4 | 8 5 6 
------+-------+------
9 6 1 | 5 3 7 | 2 8 4 
2 8 7 | 4 1 9 | 6 3 5 
3 4 5 | 2 8 6 | 1 7 9

🛒 4.  Product Scraper
This repository contains a beginner-friendly Java console project that demonstrates web scraping, data extraction, and file handling using JSoup.

📌 Project
Product Scraper

Scrapes product information (name, price, rating, link) from online shopping websites.

Saves extracted data into a CSV file for easy analysis.

Demonstrates JSoup for HTML parsing, loops, conditionals, and file writing.

Handles network connection and basic error checking.

✅ Example:

Product Name: Apple iPhone 14
Price: ₹79,900
Rating: 4.5
Link: https://www.example.com/product/apple-iphone-14
